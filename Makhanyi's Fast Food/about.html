About Us

Welcome to Makhanyi's Fast Food!

We take pride in serving the best fast food ekasi, made with fresh ingredients for a flavorful experience.

Our journey began with a passion for delicious, affordable meals that bring joy to our customers. From classic kotas and kwaggas to fries, amagwinya, and refreshing drinks, we have something for everyone.

To make ordering easier, we accept call-in orders for freshly prepared meals, ready for collection.

We’re open seven days a week, from 8 AM to 8 PM. Visit us for mouth-watering meals and excellent service—we can't wait to serve you!
